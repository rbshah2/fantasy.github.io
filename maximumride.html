Overview

Maximum Ride is a mutant, rasied in a dog crate inside of a lab. She is an avian-american, 98% human and 2% bird. She trusts nobody besides her flock: Fang, Iggy, Gasman, Nudge, and Angel.

When they finally escaped from the lab, they lived in safety for four years but of course that cannot last. While on the run, Max uncovers a secret...she is destined to save the world.

Who are the heroes? And who are the villians?
