⚡ Harry Potter ⚡

Overview

Harry Potter has always been ordinary. His aunt, uncle, and cousin think of him as weird. But, on his 11th birthday, a man named Hagrid comes and tells him that he in fact is a wizard.

When he attends wizard school, Hogwarts, Harry finds friends...and danger. Can he stop the rising force that had murdered his parents.
